Hydroponics Community Garden Project

Using mobile app technology to capitalize on real-time data, students will design a mobile app that will allow users to quickly and easily account for teachers and students in the immediate aftermath of a tornado or other disaster. The ultimate goal for the App is to allow school officials to account for and then reunify students effectively and efficiently with their parents after a tornado. The app will:
Have different levels of access for different users
Allow teachers to account for students’ status: injured, not injured, missing
Allow teachers and officials to access a real-time meeting space for updates and concerns
Provide a log of events and/or entries for officials to later analyze and use
