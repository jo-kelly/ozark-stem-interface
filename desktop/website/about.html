What is the Ozark STEM Interface

The Ozark STEM Interface is a web portal that uses an algorithm to match STEM professionals with students of all ages. Once you answer some questions regarding your interests, Ozark STEM Interface will be able to match you up with a group that is needing expertise. There are two different types of users.

  • Teacher / Facilitator
    - Creates projects for STEM professionals to join
    - Group students together with professionals
  • STEM Professionals
    - Joins projects based on questions they answered.
    - Are matched with (perfect) candidates for matching.

STEM Professionals

STEM professionals must answer some basic questions that are then fed to the websites algorithm.

Questions

  • What age children would you be willing to work with?
  • What fields of expertise do you have?
  • Are you willing to meet in person? If not what form of on-line communication would you be willing to use?

Once our algorithm has these answers, it works out the best possible student group to match with you based on what you answered in the questionnaire. You also have to fill out a form related to background-checking. The form asks for basic information (name, age, email, phone number, etc.)
